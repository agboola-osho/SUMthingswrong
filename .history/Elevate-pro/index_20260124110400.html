Team #32112

Our First Tech Challenge team, SuMthingS Wrong, from Surrey Maths School, is a rookie team to FTC. Although we have individuals with different levels of experience in robotics, we are united by a shared passion, strong work ethic, and drive to succeed in FTC. Despite being a new team, we already have a diverse set of skills: ranging from CAD and code to fundraising and outreach. We’ll be sharing and documenting our progress throughout the season here, so stay tuned for frequent updates on our robot and what we have planned for the future!

Blog

We'll record all major milestones and key events in our journey competing in the 2025 FTC Competition here!

The Beginning!

10/10/25

October 10th marked the start of our first official Robotics Club Meeting of the 2025/2026 season of the FTC Competition where we introduced the competition to all of our members and discussed the rules, aims and purpose of the challenge. We were immediately hard at work establishing our first mock up designs of what we envisioned the shooter mechanism to behave like, it was exciting to say the least!

Woking Scrimmage

06/12/25

We attended our first official Robotics Scrimmage as a School and team on the 6th December 2025 at Gordon’s School. It was an amazing opportunity to collaborate with other teams competing in the First Tech Challenge and gauge how fierce our competition will be. It was a day of many firsts as we got to see what the arena we’d be competing in looks like and navigate our robot around having our first experience of moving in the arena. It was a really important learning experience as we learnt from several other experienced teams and were able to take lots of inspiration when considering what features to implement in our own robot in the future.

The Equipment Arrived!

20/01/26

After a lot of fundraising to order the equipment, and a long time of it being stuck at customs, the robotics kit has finally arrived.

We're proud to have a strong relationship with our school as they helped us begin our journey supporting us in everything to providing equipment, a space for us to work in and access to premimum supplies and tools such as a 3D printer. ACCU Robotics




SuMS Robotics is proud to announce ACCU as our very first official sponsor, marking an exciting milestone in the development of our team. ACCU is a leading supplier of high-quality precision engineering components, and their support is already making a real difference to our work. Through this sponsorship, we are able to access essential components that will help us design, build, and test our robots to a much higher standard than before. As a student-led team, securing our first sponsor is a huge achievement and a strong vote of confidence in what we are building. ACCU’s support allows us to focus more on innovation, problem-solving, and hands-on engineering experience, which are at the heart of SuMS Robotics. We are incredibly grateful to ACCU for supporting us at this early stage and look forward to growing our partnership as the team continues to develop. More information about Accu and their engineering components can be found at:

https://www.accu.co.uk/
